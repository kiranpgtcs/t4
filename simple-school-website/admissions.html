How To Apply

A simple guide to our admissions and student enrollment process.

01

Submit Inquiry

Fill out the online inquiry form below to share parent details, child's grade level, and specific academic interests.

02

Campus Visit

Arrange an in-person campus tour, explore our state-of-the-art labs, and hold an interview with our admissions coordinator.

03

Assessment

Prospective students attend a light cognitive assessment or placement evaluation to establish language and math baselines.

04

Enrollment

Receive formal offer letter, complete documentation, arrange payment of registration fees, and welcome your child to Apex!

Tuition & Fees (2026 - 2027)

Transparent fee structures detailing yearly tuition, technology resources, and registration details.

Grade Level Annual Tuition Tech & Lab Fee Enrollment Fee (One-Time)
Primary (Kindergarten - Grade 5) $12,500 $450 $1,200
Middle School (Grades 6 - 8) $14,800 $600 $1,200
High School (Grades 9 - 12) $17,500 $850 $1,500

Additional Note

Annual tuition includes textbooks, library resources, and digital learning portal accesses. Sibling discounts of 10% are applicable starting from the second enrolled child. Flexible quarterly payment plans are available upon request.
