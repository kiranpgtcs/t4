Curriculum & Grade Levels

Explore our academic progression from early years foundation up to advanced college preparatory levels.

Nurturing Early Foundations

Our primary curriculum emphasizes core literacy, numeracy, and fine motor skills. We utilize hands-on materials and exploratory play to inspire a natural passion for study.

  • Inquiry-based foundational math and reading programs.
  • Dual-language introduction (Spanish / Mandarin).
  • Hands-on creative arts, crafts, and physical play.
  • Small classroom sizes for tailored instruction.
Admissions Info

Fostering Critical Inquiry

During middle school, we challenge students to transition from literal understanding to deep conceptual synthesis. Collaborative research and technology integration start here.

  • Integrated science and introductory programming.
  • Debate, creative writing, and historical analysis.
  • Visual and performing arts electives (band, theater, digital art).
  • Guided social-emotional development modules.
Admissions Info

Preparatory Rigor & Leadership

High schoolers follow college preparatory tracks. We combine Advanced Placement (AP) courses with internship opportunities and guidance counseling to shape future global leaders.

  • Advanced Placement (AP) tracks in math, sciences, and history.
  • College counseling, resume planning, and mock interviews.
  • Independent senior design projects and thesis writing.
  • Varsity athletics and student government representation.

World-Class Facilities

Our campus is built to fuel exploration, creativity, and peak physical development.

Innovation & Science Wing

Equipped with computerized microscopes, chemical fume hoods, 3D printers, and custom VR learning stations for physics and anatomy classes.

Interactive Media Center

Housing over 25,000 prints, subscription research indexes, quiet collaboration glass cubicles, and a dedicated multi-media podcast studio.

Sports Complex & Fields

Featuring a full-sized professional turf football/soccer field, running tracks, indoor basketball courts, and an Olympic-sized heating pool.
